Creative and Keep Exploring

Hi, I'm Huiyan Wang, an illustrator and motion designer based in London.
I have great passion for creating beautiful and meaningful illustrations and make them live through motion design. I can work on projects from scratch to finish, working on everything from the initial conceptual phase right the way through to the styleframe design and final animation.
In my spare time, I like to draw colourful illustrations with grainy texture to express the world in my eye as well.
